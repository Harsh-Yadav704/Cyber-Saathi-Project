ATTACKS TYPES :-

Cyber attacks come in various forms, each with its own methods and objectives. Here's a brief description of some common types:

Malware
Malicious software designed to infiltrate or damage a computer system. This includes viruses, worms, Trojans, ransomware, and spyware.
Phishing
An attempt to trick individuals into providing sensitive information, such as passwords or credit card numbers, by posing as a trustworthy entity in electronic communication.
Denial-of-Service (DoS)
Overloading a system, server, or network with excessive traffic, rendering it unavailable to legitimate users.
Man-in-the-Middle (MitM)
A scenario where an attacker intercepts communication between two parties, often to eavesdrop or alter the data being transmitted.
SQL Injection
Exploiting vulnerabilities in web applications to inject malicious SQL code into a database query, enabling attackers to manipulate or extract data.
Ransomware
Malware that encrypts files or locks users out of their systems, demanding payment (usually in cryptocurrency) for decryption or restoration of access.
